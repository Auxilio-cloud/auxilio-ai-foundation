Frequently asked questions

What do I need to get started?+
Just your business details, logo (if you have one), and any content or photos. We guide you through everything in a free 15-minute call. No technical knowledge needed.
What does "technical SEO setup" mean?+
We handle the foundations that help Google find and index your site — page titles, meta descriptions, fast loading speeds, mobile-friendly design, and structured data markup. This is the technical groundwork. Ongoing SEO services like keyword strategy, content marketing, or link building are separate and available as an add-on if you need them.
What's included in Monthly Care content updates?+
Each month you can request up to 2 content changes — things like updating a phone number, swapping a photo, tweaking text on a page, or adding a seasonal offer. Quick, practical updates to keep your site current. Need something bigger like a new page or a redesign? We'll quote that separately so there are no surprises.
Can I update the site myself?+
Your site is built for speed and performance, not as a drag-and-drop builder. Most clients prefer to send us changes and we take care of it (included in Monthly Care). If self-editing is important to you, let us know on the call and we can discuss options.
What does "booking widget setup" include?+
We connect a booking tool like Calendly or Cal.com to your site so visitors can book appointments directly. You manage your availability through the booking tool itself — we handle the website integration. The booking tools themselves have generous free plans.
Can you migrate my existing website?+
Yes. We handle the full migration — content, images, domain transfer. Zero downtime. Most migrations complete in under a week.
Do I need the Monthly Care plan?+
Optional but recommended. Websites need regular security patches, hosting management, and the odd content tweak. The care plan covers all of that so you can focus on running your business. Cancel anytime — no contract.
What if I want to leave?+
You own your site — the code, the content, everything. Want to take it to another host? No problem, we'll hand over the files. Monthly Care cancels anytime with no penalty.
How is this so affordable?+
AI-assisted workflows let us work 5x faster than traditional agencies. We pass those savings directly to you — no markup, no fluff.
